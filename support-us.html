Over the years many volunteers from different organizations have engaged with Vidya & Child. The volunteers have engaged through individual activities, group activities and organization led activities in different ways. We believe that this has not only benefited Vidya & Child but has also helped volunteers get engaged in the development sector.

Group Volunteering Activities


  • Career Counseling - Conducting Career Counseling Sessions for children of Class IX and above - Group of volunteers with children at Vidya & Child. This gives a very good opportunity for children to get exposed to real life experiences.

  • Excursions - Organization can help us organize one day excursions for a large group of children where the excursion can be managed and run completely by volunteers. This exercise is a very good way of bringing depth in relationship that volunteers have with our children. It also gives our children a great opportunity to get exposure to the outside world. The excursions can be for education purpose or for fun.

  • Conducting Sports Events (eg. Sports Day)

  • Guest Lectures - Group of volunteers can arrange for guest sessions lectures that senior executives at your organization or other organizations can take up for children on some pre-defined topics.

  • Reading Programme - A group of volunteers can make a weekly visit for a specified duration and conduct reading programme for children where in volunteers who know good English and reading skills can help children with reading of books, correcting their pronunciations, diction, expression etc. This can culminate into a short showcase by the children.

  • Participation of children in organizational events - Our children can give performances/exhibitions during events in corporate on the occasion of Diwali / Christmas/Holi Celebration/Annual Meet etc.

Individual Volunteering Activities


We believe in a customized approach towards volunteer engagement. It is important to let the volunteer understand the organization, children and then decide what he or she wants to do basis his / her interest area and priorities of organization. This is a first step towards creating a long term engagement with volunteers.
Individual Volunteering Options
1. Teaching Academic Subjects
2. Teaching creative activities/ art or performing art forms
3. Content Writing for academic subjects
1. Creation of worksheets for different subjects
2. Creation of Test papers and assessments for different subjects
3. Innovative content for teaching different subjects
4. Database Design (student database, volunteer database etc.)
5. Participating in fund raising campaigns for increasing awareness about the organization
6. Content writing for Spoken English from class Nursery to VI
7. Compilation of stories for: (English, Hindi)
Nursery and KG children that conveys values, basic behavioral attitudes etc.
8. Content for Life Skills, Soft Skills, Behavior & attitude related aspects for class I to V
9. Soft Skill content relevant for children of class VII to XII
10. Conceptualizing, designing and implementing activities for evaluation or practical
implementation of Spoken English and Personality development related aspect (eg. Public speaking events)
11. Mentoring a group of children from Class IX onwards.
12. Social Awareness / Academic Projects
